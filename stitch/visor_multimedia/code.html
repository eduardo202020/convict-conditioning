FIELD NOTES: THE PERFECT LINE

The full pushup is the benchmark for upper body integrity. Unlike the "volume" focus of modern fitness, the manual demands a 2-second eccentric phase followed by a 1-second pause at the lowest point.

Observe the heels; they must remain pressed back as if against a wall. This tension bridges the gap between upper and lower body power.
